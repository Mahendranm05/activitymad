Communicating via SMS and Emails

An Introduction to Digital Communication

What is SMS?

SMS stands for Short Message Service. It allows for the exchange of short text messages over mobile networks.

What is Email?

Email is a method of sending messages electronically over the internet. It supports sending text, attachments, and multimedia.

Advantages of SMS

Quick, Simple, Effective. SMS is instant and requires no internet connection.

Advantages of Email

Email supports formal communication, file attachments, and is useful for longer messages and professional contexts.

Conclusion

SMS and Email serve different purposes in communication. SMS is ideal for short and urgent messages, while email is perfect for detailed and professional exchanges.
